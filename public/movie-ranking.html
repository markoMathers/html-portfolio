The Best Movies According to Mark

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Hitch

This film is about love and meetings.

Leon: The Professional

This film is about the killer Leon. Once upon a time a small girl Mathilda meets Leon. After that they started to work together.

Perfect Strangers

3.5 couples meet for dinner party. The men have known each other for decades. During dinner they play a game of sharing texts, calls, e-mails etc.
